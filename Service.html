Service & Leadership

My commitment to service extends beyond the classroom. I believe in leveraging my skills to uplift others and build stronger communities, whether through one-on-one mentorship, professional organizations, or collaborative university initiatives.

Featured Experience: Peer Mentorship

Peer Mentor, National Honor Society (May 2021 – May 2023)

For two years, I dedicated myself to mentoring high school underclassmen, focusing on demystifying challenging STEM subjects like calculus, physics, and computer science. My goal was not just to help students improve their grades, but to build their confidence and foster a genuine interest in technology and science. I developed tailored tutoring plans and worked with each student to overcome their specific obstacles, creating a supportive and encouraging learning environment.

  • Successfully mentored and tutored over 20 students throughout my tenure.
  • Contributed to an average grade improvement of a full letter grade among the students I worked with.
  • Developed strong communication and leadership skills by adapting my teaching style to individual learning needs.

Community & Professional Engagement

National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE), Texas A&M Chapter

As a member of NSBE, I am part of a community dedicated to increasing the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically and make a positive impact. I actively participate in networking events with industry professionals and support our chapter's community outreach initiatives, which focus on promoting STEM education to local youth.

Aggie Coding Club & Aggie Data Science Club

Beyond formal projects, I contribute to the vibrant tech community at Texas A&M. Through these clubs, I engage in peer-to-peer coding sessions, participate in hackathons that often tackle social or community challenges, and share knowledge in workshops. This involvement helps create a collaborative ecosystem where students can learn from each other and grow their skills together.

My Commitment to Future Service

I am committed to carrying this spirit of service forward in my professional career. I plan to continue giving back by:

  • Mentoring early-career professionals and students from underrepresented backgrounds in technology.

I believe that technology has the power to create positive change, and I am dedicated to being a part of that movement.
